About

Welcome to ChemWeb, your go-to platform for all things chemistry! Here, you can explore the periodic table, learn about chemical elements, and test your knowledge with quizzes.


Our periodic table is interactive and accessible, providing detailed information about each element, including its properties, uses, and more. You can easily navigate through the elements and discover fascinating facts about the building blocks of matter.

In addition to the periodic table, ChemWeb offers a variety of quizzes to challenge your understanding of chemistry concepts.


Join us on this exciting journey through the world of chemistry.

Chemdle

Chemdle is a compound guessing game. Draw your guess and press submit. Red answers are incorrect, blue are intermediates, and green is correct.

Play!

Chemidle

Chemidle is a formula guessing game where players have 6 attempts to guess the chemical equation of the day. After each guess, the color of the tokens will indicate the target chemical equation: grey indicates not exist; yellow indicates existence but in wrong place; green indicates correctness.

Play!

Matchmaker

Matchmaker is a matching game where you combine certain elements based on the prompt to produce the correct chemical compound.

Play!

Elemingle

Elemingle is a fun and educational game that helps players learn about chemical elements and their properties.Every game starts with a full deck of cards, one for each element of the periodic table. Take a look at the top card and swipe it left, right, or up depending on whether you think the Mystery Element has a higher or lower atomic number, or if you've found the perfect match. Your goal is to find the Mystery Element while making as few mistakes as possible!

Play!

Elementle

Elementle is a game where players try to guess the element correctly by typing guesses into the search bar. Each attempt shows whether the letters of the elementâ€™s symbol are correct, yellow indicates existence but in wrong place; green indicates correctness.

Play!

Periodle

Periodle is a word guessing game inspired by Wordle. But instead of guessing a 5-letter word consisting of any of the letters from A to Z, you have to guess a word spelled from 5 elements of the periodic table from Hydrogen to Oganesson. Words you can enter can thus vary in length from 5-10 letters, as long as they can be spelled from elements of the periodic table and are valid English words.

Play!
